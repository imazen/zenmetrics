## What changed

Ticks 90‚Äì96 stacked these fusions and structural changes on top
of tick 89's baseline:

1. **tick 91**: `subtract_weber_3ch_kernel` ‚Äî 4‚Üí1 launch per
   non-baseband level in the weber pyramid build.
2. **tick 92**: `pu_blur_h_3ch_kernel` + `pu_blur_v_3ch_scaled_kernel`
   ‚Äî 9‚Üí2 launches per non-baseband masking level (pre-existing
   `weight_band_kernel √ó 3` folded into the v-pass scale).
3. **tick 93**: `csf_apply_6ch_kernel` ‚Äî 2‚Üí1 launch per
   non-baseband level (REF + DIST share the per-pixel LUT
   bracket math + the per-channel scale).
4. **tick 94**: `diff_abs_3ch_kernel` ‚Äî baseband bypass moved to
   GPU so every level's D plane lives in `d_scratch.d[k][c]`.
5. **tick 95**: `compute_dkl_jod` reduces via GPU
   `pool_band_kernel` instead of host `lp_norm_mean`.
6. **tick 96** (this commit): `compute_dkl_d_bands` split into
   `_dispatch_d_bands_into_scratch` + readback wrapper.
   `compute_dkl_jod` skips the wrapper ‚Äî saves the ~432 MB host
   readback at 12 MP.

The 17% JOD win lands almost entirely from tick 96's readback
drop. Earlier ticks' fusions are launch-count wins (mostly
neutral on wall-time at 12 MP since launch overhead is already
amortized at that size), but should compound at small image
sizes where launch overhead dominates.

## Open observations

- `d_bands` ‚àí 2√óweber = 645 ms is still the dominant cost. That
  bucket is now mostly the per-band readback inside
  `compute_dkl_d_bands` proper (which `compute_dkl_jod` no longer
  pays). For the JOD path, the equivalent bucket is much smaller:
  `jod ‚àí 2√óweber` = 588 ms, still mostly CSF + masking dispatch.
- The 1 MP and 256 KP launch-count wins from ticks 91-93 should
  be measurable on `examples/time_12mp.rs` if we add smaller-size
  variants. Future tick.
