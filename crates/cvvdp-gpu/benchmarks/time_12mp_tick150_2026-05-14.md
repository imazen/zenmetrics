## What changed

Tick 150 (`8c5b96e0`) replaces the wasted-Vec call:

  let _ = self.compute_dkl_weber_pyramid(dist_srgb)?;

with the dispatch-only path:

  let dist_log_l_bkg_dests: Vec<_> = self.weber_scratch.iter()
      .map(|s| s.log_l_bkg.clone()).collect();
  let _ = self._dispatch_weber_pyramid_gpu(dist_srgb, &dist_log_l_bkg_dests)?;

The wrapper version allocated ~240 MB of host Vecs (bands +
log_l_bkg) and issued `client.read_one` calls that blocked on
the GPU dispatch before transferring bytes home. Calling the
private dispatch helper directly skips the allocation AND the
GPU→host transfer.
